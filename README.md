(Full on Google Gemini) # A.M Duo Editor ⚡

A premium, fully standalone browser-based editor designed specifically for **Alight Motion XML Projects**. 

Built for anime editors and motion designers, this tool dramatically speeds up your workflow by allowing you to easily manage text categories, unlink duplicate text layers, and magically copy/paste shake effects across completely different XML projects.

## ✨ Features

### 📌 Smart Category Pinning (Powered by Fuse.js)
No more scrolling through hundreds of text layers to find your categories! 
- The editor automatically detects exactly 64 essential categories (e.g., `Strength`, `Speed`, `Battle IQ`, `Writing`, `Feats`, etc.).
- **Typo & Formatting Immune:** It uses intelligent fuzzy matching. Whether your XML says `<Strength>`, `[S p e e d]`, or `Batle Intelligence` (typo), the editor immediately recognizes it and pins it securely to the top of your screen.
- **Dockable "Other Texts":** All irrelevant layers (watermarks, background text) are pushed to the bottom and can be collapsed into a sleek accordion to keep your workspace clean.

### 👯 Duo Pairs (Linked vs. Unlinked Editing)
Alight Motion often requires duplicating text (e.g., a "Text" layer and a "Text Copy" layer for drop shadows). The Duo Editor automatically pairs these together!
- **🔗 Linked Mode:** Type once, and the editor seamlessly applies your new text to *both* layers.
- **💔 Unlinked Mode:** Splinter the pair into two distinct input boxes to edit the top layer and the shadow layer completely independently. 

### ✨ Cross-Project Effects Clipboard
Tired of manually copying effects layer by layer? 
1. **Drop an Effects Preset XML** into the editor and click "Copy Effects".
2. **Drop your Main Project XML** into the editor (your clipboard survives the transfer!).
3. **Paste** those exact shake effects directly onto your video/image media layers.

### 💎 Premium Glassmorphic UI
- Built with a modern, frosted-glass design aesthetic.
- Features extremely satisfying micro-animations, staggered slide-in transitions, and responsive hover states.
- 100% Client-Side. No servers, no lag, zero setup. Just open the HTML file.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Open the Tool:** Double-click `am-duo-editor.html` to open it in your browser (Chrome, Edge, Opera GX, etc.).
2. **Load an XML:** Drag and drop your `.xml` file anywhere on the screen.
3. **Edit Texts:** 
   - Your categories will be pinned at the top. 
   - Use the `🔗/💔` buttons to toggle how you edit pairs.
   - Use the **Bulk Replace** tool to change multiple texts instantly.
4. **Copy/Paste Shakes:** Switch to the `Effects & Media` tab to transfer effects between layers.
5. **Export:** Click the green **💾 Export Modified XML** button. The file will download instantly, ready to be imported back into Alight Motion!
